TAREK AL AMAL for General Contracting, (TAGECO JSC), is a Libyan registered joint stock company, operating in the construction sector.

TAGECO JSC was established in accordance with the commercial law No. (21) and the General Popular Committee Decision No. 443/2006, between Greek and Libyan individuals, having over 25 years of experience in the Libyan construction industry.

The company owns a considerable number of construction equipment, including crushers, batching plants, earthmoving, paving and incidental equipment. Most of the equipment is less than five years old and allows the company to participate in substantial infrastructure projects, having a backlog in the range of 215 mil LD.

TAGECO JSC is certified according to ISO 9001:2000 and OHSAS 18001:2007, demonstrating the strong management's commitment to both quality assurance and health & safety.
